IT Project Manager

Reference Number: ITPM01

Position Title: IT Project Manager

Brief Description: The IT Project Manager will be responsible for overseeing and managing IT projects from inception to completion, ensuring they are delivered on time, within budget, and meet the required quality standards.

Salary Range: $90,000 - $130,000

Reports to: Director of IT Operations

Key Responsibilities

  • Define project scope, goals, and deliverables that support business goals in collaboration with senior management and stakeholders.
  • Develop full-scale project plans and associated communication documents.
  • Coordinate internal resources and third parties/vendors for the flawless execution of projects.
  • Manage the project budget, ensuring cost-effective allocation of resources.
  • Track project performance, specifically to analyze the successful completion of short and long-term goals.
  • Ensure that all projects are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget.

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes

Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of experience in IT project management.
  • Proven working experience as an IT Project Manager in the information technology sector.
  • Solid technical background, with understanding or hands-on experience in software development and web technologies.
  • Excellent client-facing and internal communication skills.
  • Strong organizational skills, including attention to detail and multi-tasking skills.

Preferable

  • PMP, PRINCE2, or similar project management certification.
  • Experience with project management software tools, methodologies, and best practices.
  • Experience in managing projects related to cloud computing, cybersecurity, or data analytics.

Help Desk Technician

Reference Number: XYZ34

Position Title: Help Desk Technician

Brief Description: A Help Desk Technician providing technical support to end-users.

Salary Range: $50,000 - $70,000

Reports to: IT Support Manager

Key Responsibilities

  1. Provide technical assistance and support for incoming queries and issues.
  2. Diagnose and resolve technical hardware and software issues.
  3. Install, modify, and repair computer hardware and software.

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes

Essential

  • Associate degree in Information Technology or related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in a help desk role.
  • Strong understanding of computer systems, mobile devices, and other tech products.

Preferable

  • Experience with remote desktop applications.
  • Strong customer service orientation.
